The Designer
"Fashion is not just cloth and thread. It is confidence given form. Every client who walks out of this studio walks taller."

Founded in Ibadan, Oyo State, Seams by Nims was born from a deep conviction that Nigerian women and men deserve clothing that truly fits — not just the body, but the spirit. With over 8 years of mastering the craft, Nims blends traditional African textiles with contemporary silhouettes, creating pieces that are as culturally rooted as they are globally relevant.
